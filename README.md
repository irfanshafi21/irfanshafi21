## 🚀 Featured Project

### [Intelligent Candidate Discovery](https://github.com/irfanshafi21/intelligent-candidate-discovery)

> An AI-powered recruitment tool that automatically finds the **best-fit candidates** from a pool of resumes, matched against a job description.

**How it works:**
1. 📄 **Upload multiple resumes** — bulk-upload candidate resumes in one go
2. 📋 **Upload a job description** — paste or upload the JD you're hiring for
3. 🎯 **Choose your filters** — select what matters most: **Skills**, **Experience**, or **Activity/Projects**
4. 🤖 **AI does the matching** — resumes are parsed and scored against the JD using NLP/ML techniques
5. 🏆 **Get your Top 10** — the system ranks and returns the 10 best-matching candidates instantly

**Key Features:**
- ✅ Bulk resume parsing (multiple formats)
- ✅ Custom filter weighting (skill-based / experience-based / activity-based matching)
- ✅ Automated candidate ranking & scoring
- ✅ Job description-driven matching (not just keyword search)
- ✅ Outputs a clean Top 10 shortlist — saving hours of manual screening

**Tech Used:** `Python` · `NLP` · `Machine Learning` · `Pandas`
